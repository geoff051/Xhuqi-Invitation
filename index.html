Get To Know Her

Things About Me

I've spent eighteen years collecting small things — songs, quiet mornings, people who stayed. This one's for all of it.

I chose Sakura Noir because I've always liked the idea of something soft surviving in the dark — a little pink refusing to disappear into the black. That's felt like me lately: still gentle, still figuring things out, a little more sure of myself than I was a year ago.

Flower
Cherry blossom, obviously
